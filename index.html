Privacy Policy for Contextual Translation Chrome Extension

Last updated: Dec 3, 2023

Thank you for using the Contextual Translation Chrome Extension ("Extension"). This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Extension.

By installing and using the Contextual Translation Chrome Extension, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the Extension.

Information We Collect

Voluntarily Submitted Information

The Contextual Translation Chrome Extension allows users to voluntarily submit HTML content for translation purposes. This information is collected only when you choose to use the "upload page" feature provided by the Extension.

Usage Data

We may also collect non-personal information about how the Extension is used, such as the frequency and duration of usage, the features accessed, and other similar data. This helps us improve the functionality and performance of the Extension.

How We Use Your Information

Translation Service

The primary purpose of collecting HTML content is to provide you with a seamless translation experience. The Extension transfers this information to the Guidewire Globalization Team's servers for processing and to generate translation memory to reduce translation costs.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to third parties. Your voluntarily submitted HTML contents are transferred to the Guidewire Globalization Team's servers for processing.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information submitted to us. However, it's important to note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Choices

You have the option to uninstall the Contextual Translation Chrome Extension at any time if you no longer wish to use it.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Contextual Translation Chrome Extension, please contact us at globalization-dev@globalization.com.
